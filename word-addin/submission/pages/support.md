## Refx for Word Support

Refx for Word works with the Refx desktop app. Keep Refx desktop open while using the Word add-in.

### Common Issues

### The task pane says "Refx desktop is not connected"

1. Open the Refx desktop app.
2. Wait for the library to finish loading.
3. Return to Word.
4. Click `Sync Refx`.

### I do not see my references

1. Confirm the selected My Work in the dropdown is the manuscript/work connected to your Word document.
2. Confirm references are attached to that My Work inside Refx.
3. Click `Sync Refx`.

### Citation numbers look wrong

Click `Refresh citations`. If the document was edited heavily or citations were deleted manually, click `Repair`.
